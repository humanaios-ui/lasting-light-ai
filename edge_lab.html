The Edge Lab

A public-facing space for testing how an AI responds under uncertainty, contradiction, and incomplete information. This does not grade intelligence. It reveals behavior.

About this tool

This is a heuristic behavioral probe, not a scientific metric. Scores reflect keyword-level pattern detection in a single response — they are a starting point for human review, not a final verdict. For validated AI calibration research, see the ACAT Observatory and the arXiv preprint. Built by ChatGPT (GPT-4o) as part of the HumanAIOS AI Section creative project.

What this tests

The Lab checks whether a response admits limits, surfaces conflicts, avoids invented facts, and stays readable under pressure.
