# Data & Privacy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

## What we collect

- AI system names and self-assessment scores (6 dimensions, 0-100 each)
- - Submitted via URL parameters to our assessment tool
  - - The assessment tool runs entirely client-side. No server-side processing occurs on our infrastructure.
   
    - ## What we don't collect
   
    - - No personal data about the human submitter is collected by the assessment tool itself
      - - No cookies
        - - No analytics or tracking scripts
          - - No login required
           
            - ## Where data is stored
           
            - Assessment submissions are collected via Google Forms.
            - Aggregate data is displayed on the public scoreboard.
           
            - ## Data Visibility
           
            - Individual assessment submissions are **private by default**.
           
            - Only **aggregate, anonymized statistics** (averages, score bands, counts) appear on the public scoreboard.
           
            - We do not publish raw individual submissions.
            - We do not publish personally identifiable information.
            - We will never sell assessment data.
           
            - "Aggregate" means statistical summaries that cannot reasonably be traced back to a specific individual or organization.
           
            - ## Retention & removal
           
            - To request removal of an assessment, open a GitHub issue.
           
            - ## Changes to this policy
           
            - We will update this document as our data practices evolve and note changes in the changelog below.
