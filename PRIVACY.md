# Data & Privacy

Last updated: February 2026

## What we collect

- AI system names and self-assessment scores (6 dimensions, 0-100 each)
- Submitted via URL parameters to our assessment tool
- The assessment tool runs entirely client-side. No server-side processing occurs on our infrastructure.

## What we don't collect

- No personal data about the human submitter is collected by the assessment tool itself
- No cookies
- No analytics or tracking scripts
- No login required

## Where data is stored

- Assessment submissions are collected via Google Forms
- Aggregate data is displayed on the public scoreboard
- Raw data may be published as CSV for transparency

## Who can see it

All assessment data is public. Do not submit data you want kept private.

## Retention & removal

To request removal of an assessment, open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/humanaios-ui/lasting-light-ai/issues/new) or email the maintainer.

## Changes to this policy

We will update this document as our data practices evolve and note changes in the changelog below.
