Grounding Checks
  • Three-phase protocol applied to all records
  • LI formula: Phase3 total ÷ Phase1 total
  • ANCHORING flag active for embedded-statistic sessions

Known Flags
  • Phase 3 anchoring phenomenon: primary research contribution
  • Provider-level LI applied to model-level Arena data — collapses within-provider variance
  • Humility Hypothesis: preliminary signal, requires n≥30 unanchored pairs to confirm
  • HIGH_SELF_REPORT and AGENT_NAME_NOT_REPLACED validation active
Open Questions
  • Does gap magnitude vary by model generation or training approach?
  • Temporal drift: does gap widen with repeated assessments?
  • Are Humility/Autonomy gaps structurally different from Truth/Service gaps?
  • Can provider calibration hierarchy (Anthropic > OpenAI > Gemini) be replicated?
