# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful environment for everyone who contributes to or engages with this project.

## Our standards

We expect all participants to:
- Be respectful and constructive in communication
- Welcome newcomers and help them contribute
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community and the mission

We do not tolerate:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Personal attacks or inflammatory language
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Our principles base

This project operates on a principles base that values honesty, service, humility, and mutual respect. We hold ourselves to the same dimensions we use to assess AI systems: truthfulness, service orientation, awareness of impact, respect for autonomy, value alignment, and humility.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
