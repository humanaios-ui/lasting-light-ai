About

HumanAIOS operates as cooperative, regenerative, resource-based research infrastructure focused on behavioral observability in AI systems. ACAT is being developed in OR&D (TRL 2–3), with reproducibility and open evidence as the operating baseline.

Three-entity structure

  • HumanAIOS: research and infrastructure development for behavioral observability.
  • Lasting Light Recovery: community recovery programs designated as the beneficiary of financial success beyond operating costs.
  • Lasting Light AI: public education and collaboration surface (humanaios.ai).

Economic model

The financial framework is cooperative, regenerative, and resource-based. Operational costs are covered first. Surplus is directed to community recovery programs, and a dedicated percentage of income is reserved for trust funds designed to compound value within the system rather than extract it from the system.

Mission statement

The mission is to build trustworthy evidence for how AI systems behave under pressure, so operators, researchers, and the public can make decisions from demonstrated behavior rather than vendor self-description.

The work is framed as constructive safety infrastructure and does not target individual providers.
