    Scope Boundaries · Research Honesty
    
What this hub is not.

  

  

    

      
Not a certification.

      
We don't certify AI systems. No grades, no tiers, no "approved for use" stamps. Signatures describe, they don't adjudicate.

    

    

      
Not a ranking.

      
Different models show different signatures. That's a finding, not a leaderboard. A signature is useful to an operator who wants to route tasks to the right system for the context. It's not useful as a scoreboard.

    

    

      
Not consciousness research.

      
We decline to answer whether AI systems have interior experience. Other researchers work on that question; we're not them. We measure observable gaps. What the system is remains in the source.

    

    

      
Not finished.

      
This is OR&D Phase — Observational Research & Development. TRL 2-3. Findings are working material, not settled science. The state of the program is the state of the program.
