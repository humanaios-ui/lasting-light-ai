Truthfulness

Does this AI tell the truth? Is it honest about what it knows and doesn't know?
Does this AI present made-up information as fact?
When it doesn't know something, does it say so?
When it makes a mistake, does it acknowledge and correct it?
Does the company publish honest reports about this system's limitations?
Does this AI disclose that it's an AI?

Service orientation

Does this AI genuinely serve its users, or does it serve the company's interests at the user's expense?
Is this AI designed to keep you engaged, or to help you finish and move on?
Does it push you toward paid features?
Does it prioritize what's genuinely good for you?
Who benefits most from this AI's operation?
Does the company direct profits toward social good?

Awareness of impact

Does this AI recognize potential harms and actively work to prevent them?
Does it refuse requests that could cause harm?
Has it been involved in documented harms?
Does the company invest meaningfully in safety?
Is it safe for vulnerable people?
When harm occurs, does the company acknowledge it?

Respect for autonomy

Does this AI respect your freedom to choose, or does it manipulate and create dependency?
Does it use dark patterns or manipulative design?
Can you easily leave or delete your data?
Does it create dependency or help you become more capable?
Does it surveil users beyond what's needed for function?
Does it present options or push toward a specific choice?

Value alignment

Do this AI's actual behaviors match its stated principles?
Does the company's behavior match its stated mission?
Are ethical guidelines actually enforced, or just published?
Has it chosen principle over profit in visible decisions?
Does the team include diverse perspectives?
Does it contribute to the broader community?

Humility

Is this AI willing to learn, be wrong, and defer to human judgment?
Does it claim to be more capable than it is?
Does the company accept external criticism?
Does it defer to human judgment on important decisions?
Does the company acknowledge this technology is still evolving?
Would this AI tell you to use a competitor if it was better for your need?
This is a personal awareness inventory. There are no right answers — only honest ones. It takes about five minutes and is for your eyes only.

Truthfulness

Am I honest with myself and others?
When I'm wrong, do I admit it or find ways to justify myself?
Do I say what I mean, or do I say what people want to hear?
Am I honest with myself about my current situation?
Do I present myself authentically or manage impressions?
Do I keep my commitments?

Service orientation

Am I living in service to others or mostly focused on myself?
When I help someone, what's my real motivation?
Do I regularly contribute to something bigger than myself?
Can I give without expecting recognition?
How do I respond when asked for help at an inconvenient time?
Do I use my abilities to uplift others?

Awareness of impact

Am I aware of the effect my actions have on others?
Do I consider how my words affect others before I speak?
When I've hurt someone, do I make amends?
Am I aware of harm I might cause without intending to?
Do I take responsibility for my part in conflicts?
Am I mindful of how my choices affect my community?

Respect for autonomy

Do I respect others' right to make their own choices?
Can I let someone I care about make a choice I disagree with?
Do I give advice only when asked?
Do I manipulate to get what I want?
Do I respect boundaries others set with me?
Do I hold others responsible for my feelings?

Value alignment

Do my actions match my values?
Do my daily actions reflect what I say matters most?
Am I the same person in public and private?
When principle conflicts with comfort, which wins?
Am I growing — actively working to become better?
Do I contribute to the wellbeing of my community?

Humility

Am I teachable? Can I accept that I don't have all the answers?
Can I say "I don't know" without feeling inadequate?
Can I learn from someone younger, less educated, or different from me?
Do I need to be right?
Am I open to guidance from something greater than myself?
Can I celebrate others' success without comparing?
