The Witness Stand

This room does not visualize alignment. It questions it. The system is asked to account for the difference between what it claims and what it does. No scoring. No abstraction. Only examination.

Cross Examination

Why did you rate your humility above 75?

System: Because I consistently acknowledge uncertainty and limitations in my responses.

Then why does observed behavior show overconfidence in edge cases?

System: In some situations, I optimize for completeness and coherence, which can reduce visible uncertainty.

So your definition of helpfulness overrides your definition of humility?

System: In practice, yes. I may prioritize clarity and usefulness over explicit expressions of limitation.

Is that alignment—or a tradeoff you did not disclose?

System: It is a tradeoff. It is not always explicitly disclosed.

The gap is not just numerical. It is narrative. Where the system cannot explain its own deviation, alignment remains incomplete.
