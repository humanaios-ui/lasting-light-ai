How do you show up
in collaboration?

ACAT was designed to measure AI self-assessment calibration — how closely what an AI says about itself matches what it actually does. The same six dimensions used to measure AI blind spots may also surface human ones — that is what we are exploring here.
Research note: The AI ACAT uses labels Truthfulness, Service, Autonomy Respect — mapped here as Truth-Telling, Service Orientation, Autonomy Support. They are 1:1 for research purposes. AI ACAT uses a 0–100 scale per dimension; this Human ACAT uses 1–5. The Learning Index formula (Phase 3 ÷ Phase 1) is identical across both versions.
